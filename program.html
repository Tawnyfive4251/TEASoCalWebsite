Educate, Demonstrate, Guide, Enable.

See how we turn on students' entrepreneurial mindsets.

  • Discover

1. Educate

Our student mentor, Raj Pabari, provides our students with 15-minute video lessons on key topics. During the meetings, these lessons are expanded on by Raj or the mentor, who presents students with more detailed information.

  • Learn More

2. Demonstrate

In Raj’s lecture videos, there is a section called “What would Raj do?” where students are presented with a problem and solution, and alerted of potential pitfalls that they could encounter. In addition, keynote speakers talk about their specific encounters with the topic and detail their decision-making process.

  • Learn More

3. Guide

Students are given tasks to do with their team specific to the lesson they recieved. When they submit tasks or say they are done, the mentor team reviews student work and shows them what they did right and what could be improved on. Overall, students are pointed in the right direction and end the program well prepared for the final competition.

  • Learn More

4. Enable

Our program is unique in its combination of lessons and a competition in one. Student teams are put head to head and are required to use the skills they picked up in order to produce a pitch deck and realistic projections that demonstrate knowledge of concepts and ideas to a panel of industry experts.
